Planned analysis modules

The first public version is designed as a lightweight diagnostic layer for creators, marketers, artists, brands, and agencies evaluating the promotional readiness of a YouTube video or channel.

Metadata diagnostics

Title, description, tags, language signals, topic clarity, and basic search/positioning checks.

Thumbnail review

Visual clarity, click expectation, risk signals, and possible campaign compatibility issues.

Embed detection

Checks whether a YouTube video can be embedded on external pages and used in web-based promotion flows.

Google Ads eligibility

Early screening for likely Eligible, Eligible Limited, or Not Eligible promotional scenarios.

Growth opportunity score

A practical scoring layer for identifying videos with stronger promotion and discovery potential.

Creator report

A readable output summarizing strengths, risks, limitations, and recommended next steps.

Current status

Public documentation and prototype specification. The free online version is being prepared.

Project direction

YOGA is intended to support more transparent YouTube promotion decisions by separating technical checks, campaign eligibility signals, and creator-growth diagnostics from generic marketing claims.
